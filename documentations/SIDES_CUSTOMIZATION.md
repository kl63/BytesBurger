## 🎯 How It Works

### **Multi-Select Sauces**
- Click multiple sauces
- Mix and match flavors
- Free sauces labeled "FREE"
- Premium sauces show price

### **Single-Select Size**
- Choose Regular OR Large
- Cannot select both
- Large adds 50% more fries

### **Price Updates**
- Real-time calculation
- Premium options highlighted
- Total shows in cart
